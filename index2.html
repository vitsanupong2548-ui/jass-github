CMSJ Bigband

Chiang Mai Jazz City

Founded: 2024 | Chiang Mai, Thailand

The CMSJ Bigband is our flagship ensemble fusing the soul of Lanna music with the power of a modern jazz orchestra.

Conceived to be a musical ambassador for the city of Chiang Mai, its core mission is to provide a stage for the incredible local talent of our city and to spark inspiration in the next generation of musicians.
